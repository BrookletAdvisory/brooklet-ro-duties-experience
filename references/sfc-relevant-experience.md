# SFC 認可相關經驗範疇

> 按申牌主體業務分類。Experience 撰寫時必須先對齊新公司擬開展業務，再判斷此 RO 的過往經歷屬於「直接相關」還是「間接相關」。

---

## 直接 vs 間接 經驗界定（以 Type 9 為例）

| 維度 | 以 Type 9 業務爲例 |
|---|---|
| **直接業務動作** (Substantive directive Experience) | asset management, proprietary trading, research, private equity, special situations，as well as experience in dealing with other alternative investments, will be considered as industry experience directly relevant to hedge fund management |
| **間接業務動作** (indirectly relevant) | sales / marketing / risk management of hedge funds |

## 引用方式

當 Experience 段需要為「某段過往經驗為何算 Type X 相關」做論證時，可在收尾句中暗引以上 Reference：

> 例：The above scope is recognised by the SFC as industry experience directly relevant to hedge fund management (see Guidelines on Competence §4.1.9(a) and the SFC Pragmatic Approach Circular dated June 2007).

— 但**正文 bullet 內不要寫出條文號**（過於說教，且 Form 6 不是法律意見）。Reference 由 Brooklet 內部 review 時自行核對，正文措辭只需與 SFC 認可範疇對齊即可。
